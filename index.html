About Us

PT Natura Wangi Nusantara is a company engaged in aromatherapy and natural wellness products. We believe that the power of nature can be a solution to improve life quality—whether for relaxation, physical health, or emotional balance

Supported by research, innovation, and a strong commitment to quality, we present a variety of products that combine natural essences with therapeutic benefits :

  • SENÉ Diffuser – A premium reed diffuser made from pure essential oils. Available in calming and refreshing variants
  • Essentials Oil – Pure essential oils for aromatherapy, massage, diffuser, and natural skincare
  • Minyak Herba Sinergi – A versatile herbal massage oil with beneficial plant extracts
